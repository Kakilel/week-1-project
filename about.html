Growing Up

I was raised in a well-mannered, responsible, and deeply religious family, where values such as respect, honesty, and compassion were instilled in me from a young age. My upbringing emphasized the importance of discipline, integrity, and strong moral character, which shaped the way I view the world and interact with others. Faith played a central role in our daily lives, guiding our decisions and strengthening our bond as a family. The principles I learned at home continue to influence my behavior and choices, fostering a strong sense of responsibility and a deep appreciation for tradition and spirituality.
